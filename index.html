About Our Practice

Identical Twin Doctors. Exceptional Care.

At Twins Chiropractic and Physical Medicine, identical twin brothers Dr. Daniel Clements and Dr. David Clements provide their practice members with the very best in family wellness.

Our practice has been serving the needs of Orange County patients since 2011, with locations in Placentia, Costa Mesa, Garden Grove, Irvine, Riverside, Corona, and Buena Park.

We are clearly patient-centered. Our doctors always begin with a thorough evaluation and a discussion of the true cause of your problem, followed by a customized treatment plan designed to get you out of pain as quickly as possible.

  • Drug-free, natural approach to healthcare
  • State-of-the-art evaluation process for accurate diagnosis
  • Gentle, cutting-edge chiropractic techniques
  • Personal injury care accepted on a lien basis
  • Warm, family-friendly office environment
